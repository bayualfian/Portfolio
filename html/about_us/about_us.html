AUTOBIO

Since I was born in Cirebon, Indonesia, on October 29, 1987, I named Bayu Alfian. On the elementary school, I moved in two schools; SD N. Rinjani and SD N. Nusantara Jaya. Until finally, I finished primary education at SD N. Nusantara Jaya Cirebon in 1999. Completed secondary school education at SMP N. 7 Cirebon in 2002. While high school education, I graduated from SMA N. 5 Cirebon in 2005. The following year, I completed the Education Program in Graphic Design and Multimedia for 1 year in PUSDIKOM Cirebon.

My father, Albert Cornelis, was a civil servant. He served as Head of Administration at the SMP N. 9 Cirebon until his death. He died in 2005.

My mother, Fatnawati, is a housewife who survive to feed her three children. Chandra Saputra, my brother who was 1 year older than me and Tri Astiyani Saputri, my younger sister who is the youngest of us. We are just finishing up high school education because education is very expensive. We are aware of my fathers retirement salary and income of my mother is not capable enough to send us to college. Even the high cost of education to trigger inflation in Indonesia.

Although I did not attend college, not that I surrender to the situation. Therefore, I and Chandra established Kartoenbitjara Indonesia Foundation (KIFoundation) in 2008. I believe, education is very important for us to make best choices; especially when I decided to become adherents of the Catholic religion. For 24 years, I am a Muslim. Since I moved to Yogyakarta, I studied the Catholic faith and was baptized on December 25, 2011, at the St. Petrus and Paulus Babadan Church, Yogyakarta; my Catholic name is Eugenius (means well born).

KIFoundation
KIFoundation is an institution that I founded to study civil society in Indonesia. In my opinion, Indonesia is a democratic country where social interaction can tolerate diversity; in our slogan, Bhineka Tunggal Ika, which means unity in diversity. However, since the collapse of the New Order regime (1998), Indonesia seems more and more unrest which it is based on the difference. Personally, I recorded it in the Jurnal Mikro.

My study focuses on education and culture. I designed a program, Akses Terbuka (2012), an open-access development program for civil society. In this case, open access is not only a public disclosure. But, its also about resource development based on democracy.
